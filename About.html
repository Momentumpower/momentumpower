Our Company

Momentum Power Petroleum (Private) Limited (MPPL) registered as distributors of petroleum products going to establish a network of retail out lets (petrol pumps) all over the Pakistan Insha-Allah. Now we are dealing in regular and frequent bulk supplies Coal of Indonesian, South African, afghan origin; High Sulphur Furnace Oil (HSFO), High Speed Diesel Oil (HSD), Light Diesel Oil (LDO), Premier Motor Gasoline (PMG), Kerosene Oil (SKO), Bitumen, Liquid Petroleum Gas (LPG) and Lubricants to national and multinational corporations including Independent Power Plants (IPPs), Captive Power Plants (CPPs), Textile Mills, Cement Producing Companies, Steel Mills, Glass Mills Building & Road Construction as well as Automotive Sector companies and a number of production units in different cities of Pakistan. MPPL marketing plans to entails the marketing of petroleum products through the development of retails outlets and its authorized agents in addition to direct sales to Industrial Consumers.
