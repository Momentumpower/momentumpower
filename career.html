ABOUT MOMENTUM POWER

Momentum Power Private Limited (MPPL) is supporting the Pakistan's energy sector. It has been established by a very experienced, energetic, innovative, and enthusiastic team. We are dealing in regular and frequent bulk supplies Coal of Indonesian, South African, Afghan origin (Local & Imported); High Sulphur Furnace Oil (HSFO), High Speed Diesel Oil (HSD), Light Diesel Oil (LDO), Premier Motor Gasoline (PMG), Kerosene Oil (SKO), Bitumen, Liquid Petroleum Gas (LPG), and Lubricants.
